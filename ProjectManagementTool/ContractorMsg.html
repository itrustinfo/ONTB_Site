1.
BWSSB implemented a MIS system to centralize softcopy submission for CP 26 and this system also facilitates the contractor to track the status of document review.
2.
All documents required to be approved as per contract shall be uploaded through the MIS system and simultaneously 2 sets of hard copies to be submitted at the ONTB office for review and comments.
3.
Once the document review cycle is completed the contractor takes note of all observations and shall incorporate the same in the design document for the submission of next revision.
4.
Contractor to ensure that revised document shall be submitted through MIS not later than 10 days as per contract.
5.
Any delay in submitting documents as per point No.2 and revised documents as per point No. 2 & 4 shall be attributed to the contractor.
6.
If required, the contractor may collect the commented hard copies from the ONTB office.
7.
For document with minor comments with “Submit for CODE B or A” approval from DTL through MIS, shall be submitted 8 sets of hard copies for CODE B or A stamping. Contractor to ensure that these documents shall simultaneously be uploaded through MIS for releasing CODE B or A stamped documents.
